About Us

Our Mission

We believe that every community has the strength to shape its own future. Our mission is to walk alongside the people of Uttarakhand's rural areas, supporting them with resources, knowledge, and partnership as they preserve their heritage and build better lives for their families.

Our Vision

A thriving Uttarakhand where mountain communities are economically secure, culturally vibrant, and where every child has access to education and every woman has the opportunity to lead. We envision villages that honor their past while embracing sustainable development.

Grassroots Approach

We don't work for communities‚Äîwe work with them. Our team includes local residents who understand the challenges and aspirations of mountain life. Every program is designed through dialogue with village elders, women's groups, and youth, ensuring our work is rooted in real needs and respects local wisdom.

Our Work

Each program is designed with and for the communities we serve, addressing real challenges with sustainable, locally-led solutions.

Pathshala in the Pines

Education

üìç Active in 12 villages across Almora & Bageshwar districts

After-school learning centers where children receive homework support, library access, and creative activities. Run by trained local youth, these spaces help students stay engaged and complete their education.

Who it helps: Children in grades 1-10 who struggle with limited school resources and long commutes.

Mountain Women's Collective

Women Empowerment

üìç Operating in 8 villages, Chamoli region

Self-help groups where women learn income-generating skills like organic farming, handicrafts, and small business management. Groups also serve as support networks for health, education, and community decision-making.

Who it helps: Women seeking financial independence and greater voice in their communities.

Heritage Voices

Cultural Preservation

üìç Across Kumaon & Garhwal regions

Documentation and celebration of traditional folk songs, dances, crafts, and oral histories. We organize culture camps where elders teach young people, and support artisans in preserving endangered crafts.

Who it helps: Elders wishing to pass on knowledge; youth wanting to connect with their roots.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Economic Development

üìç Pilot in 5 villages, Pauri Garhwal

Training in sustainable agriculture, beekeeping, and eco-tourism. Helping families diversify income while protecting the mountain environment they depend on.

Who it helps: Farming families affected by climate change and migration challenges.
